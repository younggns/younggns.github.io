Overview

I worked at Gale Cengage Learning as a UX Developer Intern during 2016 summer term. My internship project was developing a visualization tool for internal salespeople to better demonstrate value of Gale resources.

 

Process

1. Research   As a first step, I wrote up a problem statement document to figure out problems, causes, project goal and scopes. After that, I've conducted 9 interviews with salespeople and product manager to better understand sales processes, customers' behaviors, and users' pain points. From these understandings, I came up with user persona and scenario.

2. Data Processing and Analysis   In order to provide meaningful information to salespeople, I used different datasets from different teams and tried to find useful results by combining datasets.

What I've used were datasets about students' search results, and I calculated 'importance level' of each query term so that salespeople can easily recognize what to sell.

3. Visualized Prototype   For making it easier to use, I decided to develop the toolkit on the web. I designed a dashboard interface and displayed pre-processed data and also utilized Plotly to create a graph. The recommendation system was also embedded in the dashboard.

4. Validation & Finalization   After prototyping, I also recruited several potential users to validate the system. I got feedbacks from them and finalized the product by implementing more features and improving usabilities.

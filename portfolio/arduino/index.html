Overview

Weekly Tent team, project team for data visualization, organized a voluntary project for teaching Arduino and the concept of programming to junior high school students.

 

Details

 

My main responsibilities were teaching the basic concept of programming(loop and conditional statements) and helping students to write code to build their own gadgets.

 

I’ve learned how to empathize with students’ perspectives and transform my knowledge into a digestible format for them. Through co-teaching, I also improved my communication skills.

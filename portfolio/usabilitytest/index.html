Overview

I took SI 622 - Needs assessment and Usability Evalation course and conducted usability tests on the company named OER Commons. For this, 4 members of our team conducted pre-processes such as user interviews, persona and scenario creation, comparative analysis, surveys, and heuristic evaluation.

 

Process

1. Interviews, Personas, and scenarios] As a first step, we've conducted 9 user interviews and created personas and scenarios from the interview results. I took a role of interviewer and managed 2 interviews, and created 1 persona and scenario from the reusults.

2. Comparitive Analysis] We've also conducted comparative analysis for OER Commons. We found 13 competitors and divided them into five different competitor types - direct, indirect, partial, parallel, and analogous. I was respobsible for designing and drawing the result. I used Photoshop to create the result.

3. Surveys] We recruited more than 60 participants for the survey. I was participated in the process of creating survey questions, debriefing the results, and visualizing meaningful results.

4. Heuristic Evaluation] Throughout the project, we had to analyze three different services of OER Commons, and I was in charge of evaluating one of them in this step.

5. Usability Test] For the last step, we've conducted 5 usability tests. As a member, I was participated in the process of task creation, and took a role of usability test moderator for 2 tests.

Overview

I took SI 582 - Interaction Design course, and designed and prototyped a service as a part of course objectives. The main purpose of the project was helping university students to easily find proper places to live.

 

Process

1. Problem statement & Competitive Analysis] As a first step, I defined the problem for the project. Based on the problem I'd found, I also analyzed what was currently done to solve the problem, and things that I could improve.

2. Sketches & Personas] For drawing out the idea into visual format, I sketched 40 solutions to solve the problem. This activity helped me to polish my idea and concentrate on specific approaches. Also, personas I'd created helped me to define real users' problems.

3. Design Defense] Throughout this activity, I conducted QOC(Questions, Options, Criteria) anaylsis for the application.

4. Paper Prototype] With the result of QOC analysis, I've come up with a couple of main features to design. In this step, I concentrated more on the interactions.

5. Digital Prototype] This activity was for converting paper prototype into digital version and improving previous versions.
